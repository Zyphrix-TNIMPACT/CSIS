## вңЁ Animation Types

### 1. Entrance
- Fade in (opacity 0 вҶ’ 1)
- Slide in (x: -100 вҶ’ 0)
- Scale up (scale 0.8 вҶ’ 1)

### 2. Continuous
- Pulse (opacity 0.5 вҶ” 1)
- Glow (shadow 5px вҶ” 20px)
- Rotate (0В° вҶ’ 360В°)

### 3. Interaction
- Hover lift (y: 0 вҶ’ -2px)
- Hover glow (shadow increase)
- Click scale (scale 1 вҶ’ 0.98)

### 4. Alert
- Slide down (y: -100% вҶ’ 0)
- Shake (x: -5px вҶ” 5px)
- Flash (background pulse)
